IMPORTANT THINGS TO REMEMBER!
* Always print with a white margin, ~1 inch
* Bigger target is better
* Cover entire field-of-view with images
* Have at least one image that covers the entire field-of-view
* Get even coverage of the field-of-view
* Rotate the target, but never tilt more than 45 degrees
* Never bend or fold target
* Avoid targets parallel to camera
* Size of target's is 75% of the cell size

NAMING CONVENTIONS:
[width, inches]x[height, inches]_[rows]x[columns]_[cell size, inches]
